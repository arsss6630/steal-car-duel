Race, Steal & Battle - Car Action Game

About Steal Car Duel

Steal Car Duel is a fast-paced competitive action game where every match feels like a high-stakes heist on wheels. Race, collide, and steal your way to the top in this exciting car battle arena!

Game Features

40+ Cars to Unlock

Collect over 40 unique vehicles across 8 rarity levels, from street classics to legendary machines.

Real-Time Duels

Face off in intense car duels based on skill, timing, and strategy.

1P & 2P Modes

Play solo or challenge a friend in local 2-player battles on the same device.

Customize Your Style

Equip outfits, wings, hats, glasses, and pets to create your unique look.

How to Play

  • Player 1: Use WASD keys to move and control your car
  • Player 2: Use Arrow keys to move and control
  • Goal: Steal cars, avoid getting caught, and collect coins
  • Upgrade: Use coins to unlock new cars and weapons

Equip powerful weapons like bats, hammers, or even a candy cane to gain a tactical edge in each duel!

Tips for Success

Start by mastering the basic controls and timing. Higher rarity cars offer better performance, so save up your coins for powerful upgrades. In 2-player mode, predict your opponent's moves and strike at the right moment!
